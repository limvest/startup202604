## Coverage Gaps Identified
1. **Pure-play B2C solar ecommerce platforms** — Chinese S2B2C solar marketplaces from 2016-2018 (e.g., "光伏行家", "光伏宝") are not well-indexed in English or accessible without login to Chinese corporate databases.
2. **Solar consumer loan fintech** — Dedicated solar consumer loan apps/platforms (analogous to Mosaic in the US) appear to be handled by banks ("光伏贷" products from Jiangsu Bank, Agricultural Bank) rather than standalone fintechs.
3. **Independent third-party O&M SaaS startups** — Most successful monitoring platforms are OEM-bundled (SOLARMAN, SolisCloud, iSolarCloud). Pure-play independent O&M SaaS startups are harder to identify without access to 天眼查/企查查.
4. **2013-2015 era defunct startups** — Earlier wave solar startups that failed before 2018 have limited web presence; the 绿能宝 (Solarbao) case was the most documented failure found.
